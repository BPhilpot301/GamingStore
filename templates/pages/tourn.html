Tournaments

All tournaments will be held every Friday and Saturday starting at 7 p.m. and will end at 12 a.m. Although we offer tournaments every Friday and Saturday, not all tournaments are the same. Below is a list of the types of tournaments we hold on these days, at the end of the list there will be a schedule with the dates and types of tournaments being held.

Types of Tournaments

  1. League Challenges

    League Challenges are entry-level, competitive tournaments. League Challenges are a great place to start on your competitive journey. Usually these are held weekly, typically take two days to complete, are played in a Standard format, and are a step up from casual play. This includes multiple rounds with a winner being determined by the final standings.

  2. League Cup

    League Cups are events that are a step up from League Challenge tournaments. They feature an increased Championship Points and give competitors more opportunities to test their skills in preparation for larger events. These are often more challenging and involve more competitors.

  3. Regionals

    Regionals are a major event. These involve a greater number of competitors that are skilled in the game. This is the first step in the official competitive circuit. These events typically draw a large crowd. There is a chance that Regionals will not be at this card shop. The location for every Regionals Competition will be announced within 3 months of the tournament date.

Other competitions include International Championships and World Championships. Neither of these competitions will be held at this location, however your competitive journey can start here!

Prizes will vary depending on the level of competition you are competing in. Every competition will include a well-known prize that will be announced but also include a mystery prize that will only be known by the winner after it is received.

Contests

Our contests are held once per month. These contests vary; at the beginning of every month we will release our contest details, dates, and times.

Types of Contests

  1. Cosplay contests

    Cosplay events are divided into multiple categories. They range from Anime, Gaming, Movies, etc. Each contest includes different levels that are set by age and skill. The different levels and requirements can be found at the bottom of this page.

  2. In-Store pack battles

    The In-Store pack battles are simple and mainly for fun. Two shoppers can buy up to 10 packs of cards and battle each other to see who pulls the better cards. At the end of the pack battle the winner will receive a small prize. If the two shoppers decide to battle for cards, then the winner of the pack battle will not receive a small prize but will win their opponents’ cards instead.

  3. Random Drawings

    These drawings are exactly how they sound. These are weekly events that are held during tournaments. Each competitor will have their names written down and placed in the drawing pot. Every 30 minutes during the tournament there will be a drawing. Whoever hears their name called wins that drawing. If they are in the middle of a match, their prize will be set to the side to be collected after they are finished competing.

  4. Art Contest

    This contest is for all ages. These contests are set monthly and have different levels that are also set by age. Each age group can choose their own style or type of art to submit. This can include drawings, paintings, photography, sketches, etc. At the end of the contest the winners for each age group will be announced and receive prizes.

Requirements for League Challenges

To participate in a League Challenge, you need a 60-card Standard format deck, a Pokémon Trainer Club account with an attached Player ID, and be opted into the Play! Pokémon program. You must also be in good standing with the Play! Pokémon program and be prepared to follow the event’s specific rules.

Player requirements

  • Deck: Bring a 60-card deck built using only cards in the current Standard format.
  • Account: Have a Trainer Club account with a Player ID attached.
  • Rules: Be aware of and ready to follow the specific rules and format for the event.

Requirements for In-Store Pack Battles

Player requirements

  • Both individuals must agree to the battle and the prize.
  • Once the Pack Battle is over, the prize cannot be changed.

Requirements for Art Contest

  • All participants must sign up for the Art Contest in their own age bracket.
  • Anyone that competes in this contest in their wrong age bracket will be disqualified.
  • Each competitor is allowed to choose their own art style and genre.

Cosplay Requirements

  • All participants must sign up for the Cosplay Contest in their own age bracket.
  • Anyone that competes in this contest in their wrong age bracket will be disqualified.
  • Every Cosplayer is allowed to choose their own character that they wish to cosplay. However, your character must be PG13. With children present at these events, no inappropriate cosplays will be allowed, and you will be asked to leave.
  • Each Cosplayer must specify on their sign-up sheet if they are novice, intermediate, or master with cosplay. These levels will also have separate prizes.

How to Sign-Up

All Sign-Up forms will be available at the beginning of every month for all contests and competitions. These Sign-Up forms can be turned in at any time including the day of the competition.
